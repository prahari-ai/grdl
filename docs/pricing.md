# Pricing

## Core engine (Apache 2.0) — Free forever

Everything in `core/`: GRDL compiler, CFAIS engine, sidecar, sandbox, examples.

## Enterprise modules (BSL 1.1)

Everything in `enterprise/`: RITAM, KLK.AI, formal verification bridge, regulatory templates.

### Free tier
- Non-production use: always free
- Production use: up to 10 AI agents

### Commercial tier (FRAND terms — same for everyone)
- 11-100 agents: ₹10,000/agent/month
- 101-1000 agents: ₹7,500/agent/month
- 1000+ agents: ₹5,000/agent/month

### Government and cooperatives
Indian government entities and registered cooperatives: 50% discount.

### Open source conversion
Each version converts to Apache 2.0 four years after its release date.
